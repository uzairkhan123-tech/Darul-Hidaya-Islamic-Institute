About Us

Maaz Khan is a certified Islamic scholar, having successfully completed Eight Years Of Dars-e-Nizami From Jamia Sirajia, Rawalpindi—a well-known and respected Islamic institution. With a deep understanding of classical Islamic sciences, Maaz Khan is grounded in the teachings of the Qur’an, Hadith, Fiqh.

He brings with him over Six Years of dedicated experience in teaching the Holy Qur’an to students of all ages. His teaching style is known for its clarity, patience, and ability to connect with learners, helping them not only recite but also understand the meaning and spirit of the Qur’an.
